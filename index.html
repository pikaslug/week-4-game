The Jellyfish Game

You will have to guess a number at the start of the game There are four jellyfish below. By clicking on a jellyfish you will add a specific number of points to your total score You win the game by matching your total score to randon numbers; you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number The value of each jelly is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the value of the jellyfish will change.
